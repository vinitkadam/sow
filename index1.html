Udaan : A Dream Run


An ingenious idea of organizing a fund-raising marathon was proposed by some of the devoted and like-minded people at Social Wing RAIT. After a productive discussion with all the members and with the permission of our faculty co-ordinator (Dr. Leena Ragha) and our Principal sir (Dr. Ramesh Vasapannavara, Ph.D), this earnest decision was brought into execution. The main motive of organizing this marathon is to raise funds in the form of entry fees and to utilize these funds to sponsor the education and other expenses of underprivileged students for an entire year. This was not only a small step towards the betterment of society but also a beginning of a whole new era for Social Wing RAIT. Each and every member of Social Wing RAIT raised their bars and started to work selflessly to make this fund-raising marathon a huge success. From getting necessary permissions to making necessary arrangements and from publicizing about Udaan to successfully organizing everything on the day, everyone had put in their optimum effort. In the very first Udaan, around 1500 people ran the marathon and raised 1.5 lacs, which we utilized to sponsor 10 underprivileged students from World Vision India for one year(including food, water, education). Over 2500 people ran in the second Udaan and thus, collectively donated 2.5 lacs towards our noble cause. Now, as we are in the third year of organizing Udaan, we continue to strive hard to reach greater horizons and create a new success story.
