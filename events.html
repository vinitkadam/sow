Donation Drive


Every year we celebrate the ‘Joy of Giving’ by conducting a donation drive. Old clothes, books, toys, bags, shoes or any other things which are now redundant for oneself are the kind of donations that we accept. Not only students but also teaching and non-teaching staff of RAIT come forward with helping hands by making a generous donation. We give all these donated things at ‘Children Of The World’ trust to the people who are less fortunate and can only dream of having one.

Tree Plantation


With the sole motive of “Each One Plant One” Social Wing RAIT organizes Tree Plantation Drive every year with a tremendous response from students. In the year 2016, we planted 150 saplings in the hills of Kharghar. In the year 2017, we planted 250 saplings in the greens of Seal Ashram, Nere village, Panvel. We also visit these places to make sure the saplings are nurtured and taken proper care of. A step towards saving our mother earth through a noble cause.

Udaan: A Dream Run


 In the very first Udaan, around 1500 people ran the marathon and raised 1.5 lacs, which we utilized to sponsor 10 underprivileged students from World Vision India for one year (including food, water, education). Over 2500 people ran in the second Udaan and thus, collectively donated 2.5 lacs towards our noble cause. Now, as we are in the third year of organizing Udaan, we continue to strive hard to reach greater horizons and create a new success story.

Clean Up Drive


Along with the cleanliness missions initiated by our Honourable Prime Minister Mr. Narendra Modi like “Swaccha Bharat Abhiyan” in India, Social Wing RAIT carries out Clean Up Drive every year to ensure cleanliness in different places. In the year 2015, volunteers cleaned the area of Uran Beach, Navi Mumbai.

Celebrations


Social Wing RAIT visits orphanages during Diwali and Christmas and celebrates festivals with the kids. We also visit old age home every Holi and spend some time with the grandparents. We attempt to bring smile on their faces and add joy, merriment and gaiety to their life by dancing and singing songs, rejoicing by playing old games, live performances and many more things. Women’s Day Celebrations - Every year, we organize women’s day celebration with the mere intention of women empowerment. We conduct various activities on the day such as inspirational seminars, short interactive session, self-defence workshops and many more.

Teaching At Girija


Social Wing RAIT conducts a teaching activity at an orphanage named Girija Balgruha in Kharghar. Every year our teaching enthusiasts visit Girija to help the kids in their studies, in a joyous and healthy environment. We teach them not only for their better future and good grades but also to embark moral values and become a good person. Our volunteers experience the difference between the opportunities and facilities available for them but not for the kids, which helps them to becoming a better version of themselves. We believe in shaping young minds by contributing a small helping hand towards those tender and energetic kids of Girija.

Street Play


 Every year social wing conducts Street play at Nerul, Vashi, Kharghar, Panvel railway stations highlighting various social issues like caste discrimination, female foeticide, gender equality, etc to create awareness among the society. Along with social awareness, we give opportunities to the volunteers which help them to enrich and showcase their talent and boost self-confidence.

Wall Painting


Human tends to keep their serenity clean if it's is beautified. Keeping this in mind, we have beautified the wall near Nerul station by paintings emphasizing on social awareness.

JoyFest


When the technical committees conduct technical events during the techfest; We, Social Wing RAIT, organize various fun and exciting events like box cricket, selfie station, air hockey, rink football and many more. We make sure that each and every event is done with minimum expense and has minimum entry fee so that everyone can enjoy themselves. All the funds raised from these events are used to sponsor the education of underprivileged students. In 2015, we sponsored education of 6 students from World Vision India.
